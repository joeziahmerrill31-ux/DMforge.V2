    ADVANTAGE / DISADVANTAGE

    Advantage → roll 2d20, take higher

    Disadvantage → roll 2d20, take lower

    SPELLCASTING

    Spell attack bonus = Prof + spellcasting mod

    Spell save DC = 8 + Prof + spellcasting mod

    RESTING

    Short rest: 1+ hr, spend Hit Dice

    Long rest: 8 hr, regain all HP + half Hit Dice
